Digital Planners

This portfolio entry showcases my expertise in creating digital calendars and planners that are both visually appealing and highly functional. My goal in designing these tools is to simplify the planning process for users and make scheduling a breeze.

To date, I have successfully sold over 130 of these digital calendars on Etsy, demonstrating the effectiveness of my approach. Creating and selling these digital calendars has also helped me develop a strong understanding of search engine optimization (SEO). By carefully researching and incorporating relevant keywords and phrases in my product listings, I have been able to increase the visibility of my Etsy shop and drive more traffic to my products.

This experience has given me valuable insight into the importance of SEO in online marketing and has equipped me with the skills to optimize digital content for improved search engine rankings. I am confident that this knowledge and expertise will be valuable assets in future design and marketing projects.
