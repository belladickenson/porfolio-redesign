Hudl Internship Page

During my internship at Hudl, I had the opportunity to work with a team of interns on creating a new and engaging interns page for the company. I collaborated closely with a design intern to develop the layout and design of the page, and then took full responsibility for its development using HTML, CSS, Tailwind, and Hudl’s CMS.

The primary objective of the interns page was to showcase the unique opportunities available to interns at Hudl, and to encourage talented candidates to apply for future internships. To achieve this goal, I implemented a clean and modern design that effectively conveyed the company's values and culture, while also providing clear and concise information about the internship program.

Overall, this project allowed me to develop my skills in web development, design collaboration, wireframing, and content management, while also contributing to an important aspect of Hudl's recruitment efforts.
